About Light Hill School

LIGHT HILL PRIMARY SCHOOL PROFILE
Light Hill Primary School is a private primary school established in 2009 with the vision of providing quality, holistic, and affordable education to children. We nurture learners academically, morally, socially, and spiritually in a safe environment.

Since its foundation, the school has steadily grown in enrolment, academic performance, discipline, and co-curricular activities, becoming a trusted centre of learning within its community.

After ten years of growth and preparation, the school presented its first Primary Seven (P7) candidates in 2019. Since then, Light Hill Primary School has continued to register candidates for the Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE) every year, striving for improved academic excellence.

From 2009 to the present, the school has continued to expand its facilities, strengthen its teaching staff, and improve its academic standards. It remains dedicated to its mission of producing knowledgeable, disciplined, and God-fearing citizens who will positively contribute to society.

Vision: To be a leading primary school providing quality education for academic excellence and moral development.



More Info
